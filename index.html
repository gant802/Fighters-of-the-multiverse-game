Fighters Of the Multiverse

Created by Fernando, Grant and Joel

Step 1

> Choose your fighter by clicking on the image of them!

> Player 1 selects first, followed by Player 2.

Step 2

> When both players have selected, scroll down to the battle area and start the fight!

> Once the winner is chosen, click on the finisher you would like to use.

Step 3

> After the finisher shows up below the fight button, click the "end fight" button.

> The fighter's wins or losses will be updated on their fighter card now

Additional

> Edit fighters by clicking the edit button on the fighter you'd like to edit and scroll to the bottom of the page for editing

> Scroll to the bottom of the page to add your own fighters!

> You can also delete fighters but only the ones that you create
